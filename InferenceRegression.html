    Confidence Intervals

    Prediction intervals and confidence intervals are different
    It is harder to predict one thing than the average of multiple, so confidence intervals are usually more accurate

    Formula to estimate r: Standard error of estimate = √(Σ(y - ŷ)² / (n - 2))
